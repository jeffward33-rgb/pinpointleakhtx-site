Common Questions

How long does a leak detection inspection take?

Most residential inspections take 1–2 hours. Slab leaks or complex commercial jobs may take longer. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.

Do I need to be home?

Yes — we'll need access to the property, water supply, and potentially fixtures. Someone 18+ should be present during the inspection.

Will you tear up my floors or walls?

No. We use non-invasive methods first: thermal imaging, acoustic listening, and moisture detection. If any access points are needed, we'll discuss it with you before doing anything.

Do you fix the leak too?

We specialize in finding leaks — that's our focus and our expertise. For repairs, you're welcome to use any plumber or contractor you choose. We can recommend trusted local pros if you'd like.

Will you work with my insurance?

Absolutely. Our reports are built to support insurance claims — we include photos, thermal images, moisture readings, and clear documentation of findings.

How quickly can you come out?

We offer same-day and next-day appointments for most of the Houston metro area. Emergency and after-hours service is also available for active leaks.
